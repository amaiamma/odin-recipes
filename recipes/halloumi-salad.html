Halloumi Salad

This Mediterranean-inspired salad consists of grilled Halloumi cheese and an assortment of veggies with beautifully complementary textures and flavors.

The following recipe was found on the wonderful Palestine in a Dish website by Wafa Shami. Fully detailed step-by-step instructions, images, and tips are available there. Please check it out!

    Ingredients

  • 8 ounces halloumi cheese, sliced into Â¼ inch slabs
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • 3 small Persian cucumbers, sliced into coins
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 cups baby spinach (pre-washed)
  • 2 cups arugula (pre-washed)
  • 2 ounces of sliced onions
  • A juice of freshly squeezed lemon
  • Â½ teaspoon sea salt (adjust to taste)

    Steps

  1. Heat a grill pan over medium-high heat and brush with a teaspoon of olive oil.
  2. Place the halloumi on the grill pan and cook on each side for 2 to 3 minutes or until grill lines turns golden brown and the cheese is soft and melting.
  3. Place sliced cucumbers, halved tomatoes, onions and greens together in large bowl.
  4. Mix the remaining olive oil, lemon juice, and sea salt together.
  5. Drizzle dressing across the salad and toss well.
  6. Add grilled halloumi to the salad and serve immediately.
